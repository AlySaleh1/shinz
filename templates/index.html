Shinz: an easier way to file insurance claims

Insurance documents are notoriously complicated. That is why we created Shinz, a website that summarizes complicated documents into short, easy to understand words. Simply upload the document below and Shinz will output the simplified text. In case you have a question, you can ask Shinz directly using the chatbot in the bottom right corner.
